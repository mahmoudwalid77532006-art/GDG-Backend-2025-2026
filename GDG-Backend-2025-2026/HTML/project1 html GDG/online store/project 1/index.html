Avatar

Avatar is a 2009 American science fiction film directed by James Cameron, written by James Cameron and Jon Landau, and starring Sam Worthington, Zoe Saldana, Sigourney Weaver, Stephen Lang, Michelle Rodriguez, CCH Pounder, and Joel David Moore. The film is about a paraplegic marine dispatched to the moon Pandora on a unique mission.
